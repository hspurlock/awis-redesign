The Power of GoCast

AWIS GoCast is our premium weather intelligence platform designed to provide real-time weather data, customized forecasts, and analytics tools to help businesses make informed decisions with confidence.

Key Features

Interactive Dashboard

Customizable dashboard that displays the weather parameters most important to your business with intuitive visualization tools.

Real-Time Alerts

Instant notifications for severe weather events, temperature thresholds, and other critical conditions affecting your operations.

High-Resolution Maps

Detailed weather maps with multiple layers for temperature, precipitation, wind, and more with precision down to 1km resolution.

Trend Analysis

Historical data comparison and trend visualization to identify patterns and make informed predictions with confidence.

Data Export

Export weather data in multiple formats compatible with your analysis tools and systems for seamless integration.

API Integration

Seamlessly integrate GoCast data with your existing business applications and systems through our robust API.

Industries Served by GoCast

GoCast is designed to meet the specific needs of various industries, providing tailored weather intelligence solutions for your specific operational challenges.

Agriculture

Frost alerts, growing degree days, precipitation forecasts, and other agricultural-specific parameters to maximize yield and minimize weather-related losses.

Energy

Load forecasting support, temperature trends, and renewable energy production forecasts to optimize energy distribution and generation planning.

Transportation

Route-specific weather alerts, road condition forecasts, and travel impact assessments to ensure safe and efficient logistics operations.

Construction

Worksite weather monitoring, rain day calculations, and project timeline impact analysis to avoid costly delays and safety hazards.
