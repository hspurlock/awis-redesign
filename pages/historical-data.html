All Historical Weather And Climate Data Are Not Created Equal!

We provide quality-checked, real world weather observations that businesses can rely on for critical decisions.

Many free weather data sources provide satellite-derived estimates or unvetted government data that may contain errors. Even small errors can accumulate over time and distort your analytics and business decisions.

At AWIS Weather Services, we are all about real weather observations that are clean and checked hourly for accuracy and reliability.
