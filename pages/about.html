About AWIS Weather Services

Providing trusted weather intelligence since 1978

Our Story

Providing trusted weather intelligence since 1978, AWIS Weather Services has evolved from a small meteorological consulting firm to a leading provider of weather solutions for businesses worldwide.

Our team of experienced meteorologists

Founded on the principles of accuracy, reliability, and exceptional service, we've built our reputation by providing weather intelligence that businesses can rely on for critical decisions.

Our Approach to Weather Data

1

Source Selection

We carefully select data from reliable networks including the National Weather Service, Federal Aviation Administration, and U.S. Forest Service

2

Quality Standards

All data must meet our rigorous standards for instrumentation, siting, calibration, and initial quality

3

Error Elimination

We perform extensive quality control to eliminate errors in the original data broadcasts

4

Manual Verification

Our meteorologists conduct additional manual checks that other providers simply don't do
